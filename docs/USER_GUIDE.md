# 📖 User Guide

## Getting Started

### Browsing Products
1. Visit the **Home** page to see featured products and categories
2. Click **Shop Now** or navigate to **Products** page
3. Use the **search bar** to find specific products
4. Filter by **category** using the dropdown
5. Sort by **price**, **rating**, or **newest**

### Product Details
- Click **Quick View** on any product card for a detailed modal
- See full description, rating, stock availability, and price
- Add to cart or wishlist directly from the modal

### Shopping Cart
- Click the **cart icon** in the navigation to view your cart
- Adjust quantities with **+** and **-** buttons
- Remove items with the **trash icon**
- View order summary with tax calculation
- Click **Checkout** to place your order (requires login)

### Wishlist
- Click the **heart icon** on any product to add it to your wishlist
- View your wishlist in the **Dashboard** page
- Click again to remove from wishlist

### Account

#### Registration
1. Click **Login** in the navigation
2. Click **Create one** link
3. Fill in your name, email, and password
4. Password strength indicator shows how secure your password is
5. Click **Create Account**

#### Login
1. Click **Login** in the navigation
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**

#### Demo Accounts
- **Admin:** admin@shopsphere.com / Admin@123
- **User:** deva@shopsphere.com / User@123

### User Dashboard
After logging in, access your dashboard to:
- View your **profile information**
- See your **order history** with status updates
- Manage your **wishlist**

### Admin Dashboard
Admin users have access to a full management panel:
- **Overview:** Revenue, order stats, top products
- **Products:** Create, edit, and delete products
- **Categories:** Manage product categories
- **Orders:** View and update order statuses
- **Users:** View and manage users
- **Messages:** Read contact form submissions

### Contact Us
1. Navigate to the **Contact** page
2. Fill in your name, email, subject, and message
3. Click **Send Message**
4. You'll receive a confirmation toast notification
